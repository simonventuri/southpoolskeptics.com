Welcome to South Pool Skeptics

We are a new local group meeting on the first Tuesday of the month at the Millbrook Inn in South Pool to explore science, critical thinking, and rational inquiry over a friendly pint. Inspired by the global 'Skeptics in the Pub' movement, we welcome everyone — from curious newcomers to seasoned skeptics. No jargon, no preaching — just thoughtful conversation in good company.

What is Scientific Skepticism?

Scientific skepticism is a method of evaluating claims based on empirical evidence and logical reasoning. It involves questioning beliefs and ideas, testing them through observation and experiment, and maintaining an open mind — ready to revise opinions as new evidence emerges.

Want to learn more? Here are some excellent books to get started:

  • *The Demon-Haunted World* by Carl Sagan
  • *The Skeptic's Dictionary* by Robert Todd Carroll
  • *Mistakes Were Made (but Not by Me)* by Carol Tavris and Elliot Aronson
